Our Story

How we grew up, found each other, and built a life together.

Nathan Elliott Catanach

Nathan was born in Glendale, Arizona, and spent his early years moving between New York and Arizona before eventually calling Arizona home. From a young age, he developed a love for baseball, a passion that carried him through college where he continued to play the sport he loved. Today, Nathan combines his dedication to fitness and helping others through his work as a personal trainer at Club4 Fitness, while also working with Amazon.

Laura Marie Guerrero

Laura was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona, where she developed a lifelong love for space, the ocean, and exploration. From a young age, she was passionate about sports and aviation, always drawn to discovering how things work and reaching new heights. Today, she continues to pursue that passion as a software engineer at Northrop Grumman as well as getting her Masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University. Before meeting Nathan, Laura’s first little companion was her beloved kitty, Mojito Mae, who holds a special place in her heart.

Nathan and Laura both attended Northern Arizona University. At the time, Laura was working as a software developer at the university, while Nathan was a personal trainer at a nearby physio shop. Their workplaces were just across from each other, and though they exchanged smiles, nerves kept them from speaking. Thankfully, in the age of dating apps, they connected on Hinge and went on their first date on February 22, 2025. From that moment, they were inseparable. They enjoyed trips to baseball games in Phoenix, Arizona, and San Diego, California. After graduating from NAU, they packed up Laura’s trusty eight-year-old Jeep and embarked on a memorable 40-hour road trip to Melbourne, Florida—along the way adopting a cat named Megatron to join their new adventure.

The drive went smoothly and was truly a fun experience. Settling into a cozy one-bedroom apartment together, they began transforming the space into a home. They not only survived the long journey but also the challenge of assembling furniture as a team. Over time, they shared countless vacations, visiting exciting destinations such as SeaWorld, Universal Studios, Disney World, Busch Gardens, Boston, Massachusetts, and, of course, returning to Arizona for Christmas and many beach trips. With each shared adventure, their love grew deeper and stronger.

Then, on July 1, 2026, Nathan got down on one knee and asked Laura to spend the rest of their lives together. Laura said yes without hesitation, and now they eagerly anticipate celebrating their love with family and friends. They are deeply grateful to everyone who has been part of their journey. Together, as a little family, they look forward to embracing life’s next chapter with joy and excitement.
