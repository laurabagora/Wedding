Travel & Accommodation

Please note: Guests are responsible for arranging and purchasing their own flights.

Friends
Friends are invited to stay with us at a shared Airbnb during the wedding weekend.
Family
Family please coordinate directly with your respective family member to arrange lodging and travel plans.
Transportation
We are happy to provide transportation by picking guests up from the airport and providing drop‑offs as needed. Please reach out in advance so we can plan accordingly.

Important Details

Bar
There will be an open bar, but it will be limited. Please drink responsibly.
Reception Dining
Dinner will be catered for the reception, with dessert provided afterward. For those who want to keep celebrating late into the night, we’ll also have a Taco Bell run available for anyone interested.
Children
We kindly request that guests under 10 do not attend the wedding.
Day‑before ceremony attire
Bring a swimsuit for the beach day and dress smart‑casual for the rehearsal dinner.
Ceremony attire
Cocktail or semi-formal attire is encouraged. The ceremony will take place on the beach, so feel free to go barefoot or wear sandals or other beach-friendly shoes.
